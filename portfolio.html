About Me

My name is Joshua Sobel and I was born on November 25, 2001. Ever since I was a small child, I have been interested in making designs and drawings of machines, equipment, and anything regarding aerospace. Now, my goal is to be an aerospace engineer. This website will serve to be an archive of my drafting and drawing projects.


I am also a pianist, organist, and composer. I have been playing piano since the age of 3. Some of this might be reflected in my projects on this website.
